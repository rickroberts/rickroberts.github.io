I had two good dogs, sweet Bonnie and the Crazy Dingo. This page is for my Crazy, who could fly.

Crazy was my beloved blue heeler and loyal companion for seventeen years. Sweet, loved life, gentle with children, strict with cats. And an ace flyer. See the evidence.

She came to us as a hunky puppy from a kind deaf woman who rescued deaf dogs. Check Deaf Dogs Rock for information on adding a deaf dog to your family.
